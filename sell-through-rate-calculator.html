How to read sell-through

  • High sell-through: items are moving and may deserve more sourcing budget.
  • Low sell-through: inventory may need markdowns, better photos, better titles, or fewer future buys.
  • Compare by category: shoes, clothing, cards, media, and electronics may all behave differently.

Why it matters

Revenue alone can hide stale inventory. Sell-through shows whether your inventory is actually converting into cash.
