Common questions about pricing
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. Sign up and use everything free for 14 days. We'll ask for a card only when your trial ends.
What happens when the trial ends?
You'll be prompted to subscribe at $14.99/month. If you don't, your data stays safe but import and sync will pause until you subscribe.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings — no penalties, no runaround. You keep access through the end of your current billing period.
Is my data safe if I cancel?
Yes. Your data lives in Firebase (Google's infrastructure). Cancel and resubscribe later — everything will be exactly where you left it.
What platforms does this support?
Right now Reseller's Pro is built around eBay — CSV import for orders and listings. Support for Mercari, Poshmark, and other platforms is coming after launch.
Do you offer annual billing or discounts?
Not yet, but annual billing with a discount is planned. Join the waitlist on the home page and you'll be first to know.
