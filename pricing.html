Common questions about pricing
Which plan should I choose?
Most resellers should start with Professional. It includes the full reseller operating system: analytics, AI Assistant, advanced reports, export tools, and deeper business insights. Starter is best if you only need core inventory, orders, income, expenses, and basic reports. Business is for sellers managing multiple eBay stores.
Why pay for this when I already have Seller Hub?
Seller Hub shows marketplace activity. Reseller's Pro combines inventory, orders, bookkeeping, analytics, reporting, profit tracking, exports, and AI-powered business insights into one dashboard so you can understand the entire business, not just eBay activity.
Do I need a credit card to start?
No. Sign up and use everything free for 14 days. We'll ask for a card only when your trial ends.
What happens when the trial ends?
You'll be prompted to subscribe. If you don't, your data stays safe but import and sync will pause until you subscribe.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings — no penalties, no runaround. You keep access through the end of your current billing period.
Is my data safe if I cancel?
Yes. Your data is stored securely in the cloud. Cancel and resubscribe later — everything will be exactly where you left it.
