Privacy Policy.

This policy explains what Reseller's Pro collects, how we use it, and how you can contact us about your information.

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

Introduction

Reseller's Pro ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy applies to our website at resellerspro.com and our application at resellerspro.app.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, business name, login information, plan, subscription status, and support communications.

Business Data

When you use Reseller's Pro, you may upload, enter, or import reseller business data, including eBay order exports, active listing exports, income records, expense records, inventory data, analytics data, storefront information, account balances, and generated reports.

Payment Information

Payments and subscriptions are processed by Stripe. Reseller's Pro does not store full credit card numbers. Stripe may collect and process payment information according to its own privacy policy.

Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website usage and improve our product. Google Analytics may collect information such as pages visited, device type, browser type, approximate location, referring websites, and interaction events.

How We Use Information

  • Provide access to Reseller's Pro and its features
  • Store and synchronize your business data
  • Generate dashboards, reports, analytics, exports, and AI-powered insights
  • Process subscriptions and manage billing status
  • Provide customer support and respond to requests
  • Improve website performance, product features, security, and user experience
  • Detect abuse, fraud, technical issues, and unauthorized activity

Data Storage and Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to operate Reseller's Pro, including cloud database, authentication, analytics, email, and payment services. These providers may process information only as needed to provide services on our behalf.

Current service providers may include Supabase, Stripe, Google Analytics, and related infrastructure providers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information or reseller business data. We may share limited information with service providers necessary to operate the website and application, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse, or protect the rights and security of Reseller's Pro and its users.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain login sessions, remember preferences, operate the application, improve performance, and analyze traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though some functionality may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

Data Retention

We retain account and business data while your account is active and as needed to provide the service. If a subscription is canceled, data may be retained so your account can be restored if you return. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Reseller's Pro is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete certain personal information. You can submit requests using the contact information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests may be sent to support@resellerspro.com.
